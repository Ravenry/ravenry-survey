About the Survey

Fractional work is reshaping modern workplaces, providing companies and professionals with a flexible and dynamic approach to work. In Singapore, while fractional work is not a new concept, it has historically operated in a fragmented manner.

Post-COVID, this model has gained momentum, driven by the rise of communities, collectives, platforms, and small businesses embracing innovative workforce solutions. Understanding this shift is critical to navigating the future of work.
